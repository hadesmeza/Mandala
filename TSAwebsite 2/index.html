Kitties tales show review

Kittie an aspiring author tells her and her friends some magical stories as they`re sucked into the world of the books they read here they learn life lessons such as sharing, taking care of the things you own, and helping your friends out when they need a hand but ehy always come out smiling and laughing after there adventures.

episode summary
Kitties tales follows the main character Kittie and her 3 friends terrance the croc, jort the duck, and frank the hawk. The formula that most episodes use is that at 3:00pm sharp all of kitties friends walk to Kitties shed which she has restored to be her own personal library where she tells stories to her freinds, to of her firends for example frank and jort will show up arguing about something before promptly entering the story as kittie begins to tell them stories they are teleported to that stories world and take the place of the characters in that story jort and terrance will continue to argue and kittie will begin to help them in theri endeavor while in this examplle frank the hawk would be furthering the original stories plot after a couple more minutes of arguing and they make up and the group comes to gether to fight the original storys climax. AFter that they all return to kitties library laughing and smiling and leave to go back to there homes for supper kittie then proceeds to pick up the story book that they just went into and put it away she then talks witht he audience asking if they liked that story before turning off the lights and heading home and the show credits roll
